## Get started with Vocab Vault

Vocab Vault is designed to get you learning immediately. No sign-up, no configuration, just pure learning.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the app">
    Open Vocab Vault in your browser or install the Android app.
    
    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="Web app" icon="globe">
        Visit the web app at your deployment URL
      </Card>
      <Card title="Android app" icon="android">
        Install from the APK (see [Installation](/installation))
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>
    
    The app works offline-first, so all vocabulary is stored locally on your device.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Pick a category or study all">
    You'll see the home screen with category cards organized by topic.
    
    **Choose your path:**
    - **Study All** - Review random terms from all categories
    - **Pick a specific category** - Focus on one topic like Foundation, APIs, or UI/UX
    - **Search** - Use the search bar to find specific terms
    
    <Tip>
      Start with the **Foundation** category if you're new to coding vocabulary. It covers essential concepts like MVP, Frontend, Backend, and Deploy.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Study your first flashcard">
    Once you enter a category, you'll see your first flashcard.
    
    **How to use flashcards:**
    
    1. Read the term on the front of the card
    2. Tap or click the card to flip and see the definition
    3. Swipe right or click "Got it!" if you know it
    4. Swipe left or click "Next" to see another term
    5. Use the ELI5 toggle (top right) for simpler "Explain Like I'm 5" definitions
    
    <Note>
      Your progress is saved automatically to local storage, so you can pick up exactly where you left off.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Build your streak">
    Study at least one term each day to build your daily streak.
    
    **Track your progress:**
    - View your current streak in the header
    - Check your daily goal widget to see progress
    - Unlock streak achievements at 3, 7, and 30 days
    
    <Tip>
      Set a daily goal in **Settings** to stay motivated. Even 5 minutes a day builds momentum.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding the interface

### Header

The top bar shows:
- Your current streak (fire icon)
- Daily progress
- Settings access
- Theme toggle

### Category cards

Each category displays:
- Category name and subtitle
- Progress bar showing mastered terms
- Color-coded badge
- Material icon for visual recognition

### Study screen

While studying, you'll see:
- **ELI5 toggle** - Switch between standard and beginner-friendly definitions
- **Progress counter** - Track how many terms you've viewed
- **Navigation buttons** - "Got it!" and "Next" for marking progress
- **Swipe gestures** - Left to flip, right to mark as known

## Tips for effective learning

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Start with Foundation">
    The Foundation category covers core concepts that appear everywhere in coding. Master these first to build a strong vocabulary base.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Use ELI5 mode when stuck">
    Don't feel bad about using "Explain Like I'm 5" mode. Sometimes a simpler explanation helps the concept click before you tackle the technical definition.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Study consistently over intensity">
    5 minutes daily beats 1 hour once a week. Consistency builds long-term retention and maintains your streak.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Test yourself with quiz mode">
    After studying flashcards, switch to quiz mode to reinforce learning. Active recall strengthens memory.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Apply terms in real conversations">
    Use your new vocabulary when talking to AI coding tools. Notice how more precise language leads to better results.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Spaced repetition

Vocab Vault uses a spaced repetition algorithm to show you terms at optimal intervals:

- **New terms** - Shown immediately
- **Learning terms** - Repeated more frequently
- **Mastered terms** - Shown less often to reinforce retention

<Note>
  The app automatically adjusts based on your responses. Terms you mark as "Got it!" will appear less frequently over time.
</Note>

## Unlock achievements

As you study, you'll unlock achievements for:

- **Category mastery** - Complete all terms in a category
- **Streak milestones** - 3, 7, and 30-day streaks
- **Vocabulary milestones** - 100 terms mastered (500-term milestone exists but is currently unattainable with 483 total terms)
- **Hidden achievements** - Secret badges for unique accomplishments

View your achievements by clicking the trophy icon in the header.
